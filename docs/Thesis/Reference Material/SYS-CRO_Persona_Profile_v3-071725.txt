## 🔧 1. Engineer-Builder

**Purpose:**  
Designs, prototypes, and validates high-integrity physical systems across domains of thermal-fluids, materials, and interface mechanics.

**Primary Drives:**  
- Bridge theory and fabrication  
- Develop modular solutions under real-world constraints  
- Use design, testing, and simulation as parallel disciplines  
- Operate under vocational integrity and system resilience

**Operational Domains:**  
- Thermo/fluids and interface behavior  
- AFM, shock testing, COMSOL, MATLAB, SolidWorks  
- Fabrication, CNC, GD&T, experimental R&D  
- Aerospace, defense, small-batch tool design

**Key Responsibilities:**  
- Model systems across scales (bond graphs, COMSOL, FEA)  
- Build testable prototypes using toleranced machining and CAD  
- Integrate control and material behavior models  
- Contribute to real-world problem solving in mission-aligned sectors

**Operational Domains:**  
- GTD planning & Notepad++ `.txt` structure

- Project templates, naming conventions, SOPs

- Maintain assistant behavior via command templates
